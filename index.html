Who we are

The TICKETZA.com team wears many hats from programmers to account managers to data scientists and beyond. Although our tasks differ, we are united by one belief: that buses are good for communities, good for the environment, and an all around great way to move groups.
TICKETZA.com is changing the way that groups travel by making it easier than ever to book a bus online. Through our unique technology, we are modernizing the charter bus industry to provide service across India that is safe, easy, and affordable.

From its founding in 2014 to today, TICKETZA.com has evolved from a ride-sharing platform to an industry leader in charter bus rentals, working with groups both large and small.
